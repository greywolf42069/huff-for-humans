🤔 What is Huff?

Huff is a low‑level language for Ethereum smart contracts. It compiles directly to EVM opcodes — no abstractions, just pure stack magic.

⚙️ Assembly?

Yes! Every operation in Huff is an opcode like sstore, mload, add. It’s the same machine code the EVM executes.

🧠 Sovereign Stack Management

Huff gives you total control over the stack. No hidden checks, no extra gas. You become one with the machine. That’s enlightening — and saves ~80% gas vs Solidity.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions
Why Huff instead of Solidity?
Huff removes all overhead. You write exactly what the EVM does, achieving minimal gas costs and maximum transparency. It’s perfect for gas‑critical protocols or learning the internals.
Is Huff safe?
Safety comes from understanding. Huff doesn’t hold your hand, but it lets you build exactly what you intend. With proper testing (like our Troll Test suite), you can achieve safety without bloat.
Where can I try Huff?
Start with our tutorial. You can use the Huff compiler locally or the online playground at huff.sh.
What's the gas saving?
Our SimpleHuffToken uses ~12k gas per transfer vs ~55k in OpenZeppelin — that’s ~78% cheaper. Deployment is ~80% cheaper.
Is this production ready?
This repo is educational, but the patterns are used in real‑world optimized contracts. Audit before deploying any asset.
